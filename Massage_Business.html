Education

Dr. Sergey Komarov is from Tambov, Russian Federation. He studied for 12 years in Military Medical Academy and completed the necessary residency hours to qualify him for the title of 'Doctor.' He then worked in St. Petersburg, Russia as a general physician for five years, before deciding to permanently move to the United States of America. Here, Dr. Komarov studied the art of massage therapy. After studying and practicing at the European School of Massage therapy, over the course of two years, he obtained his license in 2004. Though he is not presently a doctor in the U.S., there is an old Russian saying: "There are no former doctors."
